Musical Parodies

My two great passions in life are music and technology. And I like making people laugh. So I take great songs – songs I love, songs that are fun to play and fun to sing along and dance to – and I replace the lyrics with daft jokes about software development. Then I make videos of them. Then I go along to conferences with my Steinberger travel guitar, rope in a couple of other tech folks who are musically inclined, and perform them live on stage - check out Dylan Beattie and the Linebreakers - Live at NDC Oslo 2019 on YouTube for more.
