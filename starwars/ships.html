POV-Ray Model Files

Note for POV-Ray 3.1 Users

Some of the models have finally been updated to use 'interior' instead of 'halo', since halos are unsupported in POV-Ray 3.1. These conversions are the work of John Dickson, to whom I am indebted. A few models haven't been converted yet, so if anyone wants to follow John's example and convert some of them, please go ahead, send me the converted files and I'll post them here. (The original POV-Ray 3.0 model descriptions are still included in the ZIP files for anyone without POV-Ray 3.1.)
