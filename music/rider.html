Live Music - Tech Specification

Here's what you'll need to provide for me to play live at your party or other event.

Essentials

PA system
My show requires a good-quality PA system suited to the size of the room, with no latency.
A projector and screen, with a cabled HDMI connection available from the stage.
My show is audiovisual and the videos are an essential part of the performance.

Nice To Have

If you're able to provide these, brilliant. If not, please let me know in advance and we can work around it.

On-stage monitor wedges
If possible, a feed of the front-of-house sound mix into a pair of monitor wedges.

Showstoppers

I'm generally pretty flexible and will find a way to make things work - but there's a couple of things I've come across that just make it impossible to play a good show. If any of these is likely to be an issue, please let me know ahead of time and we can work something out.

Bluetooth-based audio systems
Some auditoriums (particularly cinema screens) use a wireless Bluetooth-based audio system. This introduces too much latency into the sound signal and makes it impossible to keep time when playing live.
Wireless screen-sharing
I run the show via an HDMI connection from my Macbook Pro. I can run VGA, DVI or Mini DisplayPort, but wireless 'video casting' o software-based screen sharing introduce too much latency between the audio and the video.
Adjoining events running at the same time
I play loud rock music. Having the venue manager turn it down because our party is in Room A and there's a Bible study group in Room B next door puts a bit of a downer on things.
